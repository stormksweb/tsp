Our Philosophy

We are a close and supportive group. The goal is not just to grow stronger, but to help each other improve, learn new things, and enjoy the game together.

Consistency, teamwork, and a good atmosphere matter more to us than ego.

Who We Welcome

Whales, free-to-play players, experienced fighters, and developing accounts are all welcome. We value attitude and participation more than raw power.

All languages and backgrounds are welcome.

What We’re Looking For

Active players who enjoy the game, communicate, and want to be part of a real team.

You do not need to be the strongest player. Showing up and contributing matters more.

Why Join TsP

Supportive team
People help each other, share tips, and genuinely want the whole alliance to improve.
Active but chill
We take events seriously, but we keep the atmosphere relaxed, friendly, and easy to be part of.
Room to grow
Whether you are already strong or still developing, consistency and attitude matter more than raw power.

Recruitment

Requirements

We are looking for active players who want to be part of a real team. Power matters less than consistency, communication, and willingness to join in.

  • Join alliance events regularly
  • Read daily notices and follow simple instructions
  • Communicate if you are unsure about something
  • Bring a good attitude and team spirit
Event Rules

We keep things clear. There is a daily notice so members know what matters that day, from event times to any special instructions.

  • Show up for events when you can
  • Follow rally and event instructions
  • Ask if anything is unclear
  • Team coordination comes first
TsP Lore

TsP runs on shenanigans 24/7. The alliance chat is full of side projects, jokes, wild ideas, and the kind of nonsense that somehow becomes a real plan.

Current examples include a sexy Kingshot avatar calendar, random alliance projects, and enough inside jokes that our town crier may need to brief you when you log in.

Even with all that, we stay organized. Fun and clear communication go together here.
